# Coffee Rush

Coffee Rush game is an engaging time-management simulation where players run their own coffee shop and serve customers efficiently.

## What is Coffee Rush?

The Coffee Rush game immerses players in the fast-paced world of coffee shop management. As the owner, players must juggle brewing coffee, preparing snacks, and delivering orders to meet customer demands. The game challenges players to think quickly and make strategic decisions to keep their customers happy and their business thriving.

In this simulation, players will encounter various scenarios that require efficient time management and multitasking skills. The Coffee Rush game not only tests your ability to serve customers but also allows you to customize and upgrade your shop, making it a unique experience for each player.

## Key Features

- **Time-Management Gameplay** — Experience the thrill of managing a busy coffee shop while serving customers efficiently.
- **Variety of Drinks and Snacks** — Create a diverse menu featuring various coffee drinks and delicious snacks.
- **Shop Customization** — Personalize your coffee shop with different themes and decorations to attract more customers.
- **Challenging Levels** — Progress through multiple levels, each presenting new challenges and customer demands.
- **Customer Types** — Serve a range of customers with different preferences and patience levels, adding complexity to the gameplay.
- **Upgrades and Unlocks** — Earn rewards and unlock new equipment to enhance your coffee shop's efficiency.
- **Achievements and Leaderboards** — Compete with friends and players worldwide to achieve high scores and showcase your skills.
- **Engaging Graphics and Sound** — Enjoy vibrant graphics and immersive sound effects that enhance the overall gaming experience.
