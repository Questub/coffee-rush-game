Coffee Rush game stands out in the time-management simulation genre by combining engaging gameplay with a rich coffee shop management experience. Players are tasked with efficiently serving customers while managing resources, upgrading equipment, and expanding their coffee shop’s offerings. The vibrant graphics and intuitive controls make it appealing to both casual gamers and those seeking a deeper strategy experience.

## Core Features

### Engaging Gameplay
- Manage a bustling coffee shop with fast-paced customer service.
- Complete various challenges and quests to earn rewards.
- Unlock new recipes and beverages to keep customers satisfied.

### Customization Options
- Personalize your coffee shop's layout and design.
- Upgrade equipment to enhance service speed and quality.
- Expand your menu with unique drinks and snacks.

### Customer Interaction
- Cater to diverse customer preferences and personalities.
- Handle rush hours and special events for bonus rewards.
- Build relationships with regular customers for additional perks.
