The Coffee Rush game is an engaging time-management simulation where players run their own coffee shop and serve customers efficiently. Follow this guide to download and set up the game easily.

## Option 1 — Browser / Web App

### Step 1 — Visit the Game Website
1. Open your preferred web browser.
2. Go to the official Coffee Rush game website.

### Step 2 — Create an Account
1. Click on the 'Sign Up' or 'Register' button.
2. Fill in the required information such as your email and password.
3. Confirm your email address if prompted.

### Step 3 — Start Playing
1. Log in to your account on the game website.
2. Click on the 'Play Now' button to launch the game in your browser.
3. Follow on-screen instructions to begin your coffee shop journey.

---

## Option 2 — Desktop App

### Step 1 — Download the Installer
1. Visit the official Coffee Rush game website.
2. Navigate to the 'Download' section.
3. Click on the download link for the desktop app compatible with your operating system.

### Step 2 — Install the Game
1. Locate the downloaded installer file on your computer.
2. Double-click the installer to start the setup process.
3. Follow the installation prompts to complete the setup.

### Step 3 — Launch the Game
1. Once installed, find the Coffee Rush game icon on your desktop or in your applications folder.
2. Double-click the icon to launch the game.
3. Log in with your account or start a new game.
