## General

**Is Coffee Rush free?**  
Yes, the Coffee Rush game is available for free, with optional in-game purchases for additional features and upgrades.

**What is Coffee Rush used for?**  
The Coffee Rush game is designed for players to manage their own coffee shop, serving customers efficiently while completing various time-management challenges.

**Does Coffee Rush work offline?**  
The Coffee Rush game requires an internet connection to download and update, but it can be played offline once fully installed.

## Download & Installation

**How do I download Coffee Rush?**  
To download the Coffee Rush game, visit the official website or your device's app store and search for "Coffee Rush." Follow the prompts to install the game.

**Is Coffee Rush available on Windows / macOS / Linux?**  
Yes, the Coffee Rush game is available on Windows and macOS. It is not currently available for Linux.

**Can I use Coffee Rush without installing anything?**  
No, the Coffee Rush game requires installation to run, as it involves local game files for proper performance.

## Account & Pricing

**What's included in the free plan?**  
The free plan includes:  
- Access to the basic features of the Coffee Rush game  
- A limited number of levels and challenges  
- Basic customer types and orders

**How do I upgrade to a paid plan?**  
To upgrade to a paid plan, open the Coffee Rush game, go to the settings or account menu, and select the upgrade option. Follow the prompts to complete your purchase.

**Can I use Coffee Rush for commercial projects on the free plan?**  
No, the free plan of the Coffee Rush game is intended for personal use only and does not allow for commercial projects.

## Troubleshooting

**Coffee Rush is not loading — what should I do?**  
1. Check your internet connection to ensure it is stable.  
2. Restart the Coffee Rush game.  
3. Clear the cache of the application.  
4. Reinstall the game if the problem persists.  
5. Contact support if none of the above steps work.

**How do I contact support?**  
You can contact support for the Coffee Rush game by visiting the official website and navigating to the support section, or by sending an email to the provided support address.
